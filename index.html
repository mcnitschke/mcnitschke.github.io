I am an applied mathematician whose research broadly focuses on developing mechanistic models of biological and evolutionary processes. Currently, I am a member of the Global Ecology Laboratory at Flinders University in Adelaide, South Australia, working within the Australian Research Council Centre of Excellence for Indigenous and Environmental Histories and Futures.
